# Conditionals 

## Conditional
```p --> q <=> ¬q v p```

## Contrapositive
```¬q --> ¬p <=> q v ¬p```

## Converse
```q --> q <=> q v ¬p```

## Inverse 
```¬p --> ¬q <=> p v ¬q```
